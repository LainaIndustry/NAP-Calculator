Understanding Sleep Cycles

Why Sleep Cycles Matter

Sleep consists of cycles lasting about 90 minutes each. Waking up at the end of a cycle, during light sleep, helps you feel more rested and alert.

How Much Sleep You Need

Most adults need 7-9 hours of sleep per night (5-6 complete sleep cycles). The NAP Calculator helps you plan your sleep schedule according to these natural cycles.
